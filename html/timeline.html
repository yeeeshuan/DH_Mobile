1941: Born in a valley near Lake Constance, the Salem Valley, in Germany close to the Swiss border.

1958: Began a 2 year course at the Merz academy and learned about hand setting type and printmaking.

1960: Began a typesetting apprenticeship at Kuwe printing

1962: Began Round Composition Project.

1963: Enrolled at the Basel School of Design in Switzerland. Met Armin Hoffman and Emil Ruder.
